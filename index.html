One Happy Moment:

seeking joy...

"One Happy Moment" is a project created by Una Kravets as a practice in gratitude. Every day of 2015 she has written down one happy moment as a part of her personal goals repository on Github. It's a reminder to search for one happy moment, no matter how small, to remember the day by. This site was created by using Kimono to turn the repo into an API, and _____ to _____. The typeface used is Roboto.
